Help Menu
------------------------------------------------------------
Shuffle bot messages out a random song every day.  To get an immediate response, use the following commands:

Random Song: !shuffle
Random Song (Genre Specific): !shuffle "genre"
Top song (US and Global): !shuffle top
Top TikTok song: !shuffle tiktok
Top song (Genre Specific): !shuffle top "genre"
