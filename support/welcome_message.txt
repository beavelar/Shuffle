__**Welcome!**__
Shuffle bot messages out a random song every day.  To get an immediate response, or for help, use the following commands


__**Commands**__
**!shuffle help**: Displays the help menu
**!shuffle**: Displays random song
**!shuffle top**: Displays top song (US and Global)
**!shuffle tiktok**: Displays top TikTok song
